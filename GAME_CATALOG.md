### 1. **Crazy Eights**
- **Slug**: `crazy-eights`
- **Accent Color**: Red (#DC2626)
- **Image**: `/assets/crazy-eights-card.jpg`
- **Description**: Match suit or rank to get rid of cards. Eights are wild and change the suit!
- **Rules Summary**: Match the color or number of the top discard card. Wild cards can be played anytime. Action cards include Skip, Reverse, Draw Two, and Wild Draw Four. First player to get rid of all cards wins the round. Score points based on cards remaining in opponents' hands.
- **Player Range**: 2-8 players
- **Difficulty**: Easy
- **Game Type**: Matching, Hand Management

### 2. **Gin Rummy**
- **Slug**: `gin-rummy`
- **Accent Color**: Purple (#8B5CF6)
- **Image**: `/assets/gin-rummy-card.jpg`
- **Description**: Two-player matching and melding game. Knock when your deadwood is low enough!
- **Rules Summary**: Form sets and runs to reduce deadwood (unmatched cards). Knock when deadwood is 10 or less. Opponent can lay off cards on your melds. Score based on deadwood difference. Gin (no deadwood) scores bonus. First to 100 points wins.
- **Player Range**: 2 players
- **Difficulty**: Medium
- **Game Type**: Melding, Hand Management

---

### 3. **Go Fish**
- **Slug**: `go-fish`
- **Accent Color**: Cyan (#0891B2)
- **Image**: `/assets/go-fish-card.jpg`
- **Description**: Simple matching card game for all ages. Ask opponents for cards to make sets!
- **Rules Summary**: Ask opponents for cards of a specific rank. If they have it, they give you all of that rank. If not, "Go Fish" - draw a card. Form sets of 4 matching cards. Most sets at end wins. Perfect for kids and families.
- **Player Range**: 2-6 players
- **Difficulty**: Easy
- **Game Type**: Matching, Memory

### 4. **Hearts**
- **Slug**: `hearts`
- **Accent Color**: Red (#EF4444)
- **Image**: `/assets/hearts-card.jpg`
- **Description**: Avoid taking hearts and especially the Queen of Spades. Lowest score wins!
- **Rules Summary**: Trick-taking game where hearts are worth 1 point each and Queen of Spades is 13 points. Must follow suit. Highest card of led suit wins trick. Cannot lead hearts until broken. Shooting the moon (taking all hearts + Queen) gives 26 points to opponents.
- **Player Range**: 4 players (3-6 with variants)
- **Difficulty**: Medium
- **Game Type**: Trick-Taking, Avoidance

### 5. **Monopoly Deal**

### 6. **Phase 10**
- **Slug**: `phase10`
- **Accent Color**: Green (#16A34A)
- **Image**: `/assets/phase10-card.jpg`
- **Description**: Complete 10 challenging phases from sets to runs. First to finish all phases wins!
- **Rules Summary**: Each player must complete 10 different phases in order: 2 sets of 3, 1 set of 3 + 1 run of 4, etc. Draw and discard cards to complete your current phase. Wild and Skip cards add strategy. First player to complete all 10 phases wins.
- **Player Range**: 2-6 players
- **Difficulty**: Medium
- **Game Type**: Set Collection, Phase Completion

### 8. **Rummy**

### 10. **Solitaire**
- **Slug**: `solitaire`
- **Accent Color**: Green (#10B981)
- **Image**: `/assets/solitaire-card.jpg`
- **Description**: Classic single-player card game. Build foundation piles from Ace to King!
- **Rules Summary**: Klondike variant: Build 4 foundation piles by suit from Ace to King. Tableau builds in alternating colors descending. Turn cards from stock pile. Move cards between tableau columns. Goal: Get all cards to foundations. Patience and strategy required.
- **Player Range**: 1 player
- **Difficulty**: Medium
- **Game Type**: Patience, Sequencing

### 11. **Spades**
- **Slug**: `spades`
- **Accent Color**: Dark Gray (#1F2937)
- **Image**: `/assets/spades-card.jpg`
- **Description**: Trick-taking game with bidding. Partnership game with strategy and communication.
- **Rules Summary**: Partners bid number of tricks they'll take. Spades are always trump. Must follow suit if able. Team must meet their combined bid to score. Bags (extra tricks) penalize. Nil bids (zero tricks) are high risk/reward. First to 500 wins.
- **Player Range**: 4 players (2v2 partnership)
- **Difficulty**: Medium-Hard
- **Game Type**: Trick-Taking, Bidding, Partnership
